About DutiCalls

Transforming workforce management for modern businesses

Our Story

DutiCalls was born from a simple observation: workforce management shouldn't be complicated. In 2020, our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of managing remote and distributed teams during a global shift to flexible work arrangements.

Traditional attendance and HR systems were clunky, difficult to use, and didn't adapt to the modern workplace. Employees struggled with complicated check-in processes, managers couldn't get real-time insights, and HR teams spent countless hours on manual data entry and reconciliation.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we set out to build DutiCalls - a workforce management platform that's powerful yet simple, comprehensive yet intuitive, and accessible from anywhere.

Today, DutiCalls serves over 500 companies and 10,000+ active users worldwide, helping them streamline attendance, track productivity, and manage their workforce with ease.

Our Mission

To empower organizations of all sizes with intuitive, powerful workforce management tools that save time, increase productivity, and improve employee experience. We believe that managing people should be about supporting their growth, not drowning in administrative tasks.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted workforce management platform, enabling every organization to unlock the full potential of their teams through intelligent automation, real-time insights, and seamless user experiences that adapt to the future of work.
