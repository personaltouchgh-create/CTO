Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DutiCalls

What is DutiCalls?

+

DutiCalls is a comprehensive workforce management platform designed to simplify attendance tracking, employee monitoring, and HR processes. It offers one-click attendance, real-time tracking, multi-branch management, and powerful mobile experience for modern businesses.

Who can benefit from using DutiCalls?

+

DutiCalls is perfect for businesses of all sizes - from small startups to large enterprises. It's especially valuable for companies with remote teams, multiple office locations, field employees, or anyone looking to modernize their attendance and workforce management processes.

How quickly can we get started with DutiCalls?

+

You can get started immediately! Sign up for a free trial, and you'll have access to the platform within minutes. Our intuitive setup wizard will guide you through adding employees, configuring settings, and customizing the system to your needs. Most companies are fully operational within 24-48 hours.

Do you offer training and onboarding support?

+

Yes! All plans include access to our comprehensive knowledge base and video tutorials. Professional and Enterprise plans include dedicated onboarding sessions with our customer success team to ensure smooth implementation.

Can I migrate data from my existing system?

+

Absolutely! We support bulk import of employee data and historical attendance records via CSV/Excel files. Our team can also assist with data migration for Enterprise customers.

How does one-click attendance work?

+

Employees simply click a single button to mark their attendance. The system automatically records the timestamp, location (if enabled), and updates the dashboard in real-time. It's that simple - no complicated forms or manual entry required.

Can employees mark attendance from their mobile devices?

+

Yes! DutiCalls offers a full-featured mobile app for both iOS and Android, plus Progressive Web App (PWA) support for offline access. Employees can mark attendance, view schedules, and communicate with team members from anywhere.

What attendance verification methods do you support?

+

We support multiple verification methods including: one-click attendance, selfie-based verification, QR code scanning, geo-location binding, IP-based restrictions, area-based attendance, and integration with biometric devices. You can use one or combine multiple methods for enhanced security.

How does multi-branch management work?

+

The multi-branch feature allows you to manage multiple office locations from a single dashboard. You can assign employees to specific branches, view branch-specific reports, set location-based policies, and easily switch between branches using our intuitive branch selector.

What is the AI Assistant feature?

+

Our AI Assistant is an intelligent helper that automates routine HR tasks, answers employee questions about policies, provides insights from attendance data, and helps managers make data-driven decisions. It's available as a premium add-on or included in Enterprise plans.

Can I integrate DutiCalls with existing biometric devices?

+

Yes! We support integration with most major biometric devices. Attendance data from your existing hardware will automatically sync with DutiCalls, giving you a unified view of all attendance records.

What's included in the free trial?

+

Our 14-day free trial gives you full access to all features of your chosen plan. No credit card required to start. You can invite your team, test all features, and see how DutiCalls fits your needs before committing.

Can I change my plan later?

+

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades are applied at the start of your next billing cycle. Your data is always preserved during plan changes.

What happens if I exceed my employee limit?

+

If you're approaching your employee limit, we'll notify you in advance. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional employee licenses. We'll never restrict access to your existing employees without notice.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

+

Yes! Save 20% when you choose annual billing. Annual plans also include priority support and additional features depending on your tier.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for annual Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through industry-standard payment gateways.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with DutiCalls within the first 30 days, contact us for a full refund. No questions asked.

What browsers and devices are supported?

+

DutiCalls works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and devices. Our mobile app is available for iOS 12+ and Android 6+ devices. The PWA version works on any device with a compatible browser.

Is an internet connection required?

+

While an internet connection is required for real-time syncing, our PWA supports offline mode. Employees can mark attendance offline, and data will automatically sync when connection is restored.

Can I integrate DutiCalls with other tools?

+

Yes! We offer API access and pre-built integrations with popular HR, payroll, and productivity tools. Enterprise customers can also request custom integrations.

What is your uptime guarantee?

+

We guarantee 99.9% uptime. Our infrastructure is hosted on reliable cloud servers with automatic backups and redundancy to ensure your data is always accessible.

How often do you release updates?

+

We release feature updates monthly and security patches as needed. All updates are automatic and require no action from you. We'll notify you of major new features via email and in-app notifications.

How secure is my data?

+

We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256). We employ industry-standard security practices, regular security audits, and comply with major data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

Where is my data stored?

+

Data is stored on secure cloud servers in tier-1 data centers with ISO 27001 certification. Enterprise customers can choose their preferred data region for compliance requirements.

Do you perform regular backups?

+

Yes! We perform automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery capabilities. Your data is backed up to multiple geographic locations for additional redundancy.

Can I export my data?

+

Absolutely! You can export all your data (employee records, attendance logs, reports) at any time in standard formats (CSV, Excel, PDF). You own your data and can take it with you if needed.

Are you GDPR compliant?

+

Yes! DutiCalls is fully GDPR compliant. We provide tools for data subject access requests, right to deletion, data portability, and other GDPR requirements. We also comply with CCPA and other major privacy regulations.

What access controls are available?

+

DutiCalls offers role-based access control (RBAC), multi-factor authentication (MFA), single sign-on (SSO), IP whitelisting, and detailed audit logs. You have complete control over who can access what data.
